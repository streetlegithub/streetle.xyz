Windows 10 PCs

Download the file. Click on the .mcpack file and the Minecraft Application will launch, upon launching, allow 10-15 seconds for the mod to finish importing. Then, enable the mod in "Global Resources" in your settings or "Resource Packs" to enable it in specific worlds.

PlayStation 4 and PlayStation 5 consoles

Nintendo Switch devices are currently unable to install .mcpack files. The only current method is to enable the mod on a Realm, and then join the Realm on your switch.
