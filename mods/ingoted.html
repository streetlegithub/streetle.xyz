Ingoted


Ingoted makes your Minecraft experience a lot more finished and allows you to have better understanding of what items are made from ores and what aren't. This helps make all mineable ores into ingots once smelted. It can also work on servers such as The Hive or Cubecraft, as they use smelted ores as currency.


This mod changes Diamonds, Emeralds and Coal into ingots, as it should be!




This mod will work on any device that can run Minecraft Bedrock Edition and install mods.

This mod will be compatible with any mod that does not change the diamond, emerald or coal textures.


Why does the mod fail to import?

Try changing the file extension to zip, then uncompress it and put it in your resource_packs folder. This may work however is not recommended and should only be used as a final option.

What ingots does this change?

It changes the diamond, emerald and coal textures to be ingots, as it should be!
