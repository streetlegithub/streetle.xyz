About Me

I'm streetle, a 13+ year old boy from England. I've always loved Minecraft, so I decided to begin creating mods and even some worlds for the game for people to play around with.

I've been making these for a good couple years now, taking a lot of breaks, however I usually post at least one piece of content a month.

*I do not like to provide my actual age for privacy reasons. Stay safe.

History

I began by posting mods on MCPEDL back in 2020 during the pandemic, and ended up gaining a lot of traction from my most popular mod " FullBright".

Sadly, the FullBright mod no longer works, and has been removed from all sites I could take it down from. This is due to Minecraft releasing RenderDragon, a new rendering engine which disallows players to edit shaders.

Team

This is all ran by one person, me. I plan to add more people if I become more popular. For now, its just me.
